Privacy Policy

By using the Website, you consent to this policy.

We will gather personal data on this Website only if it is directly provided to us by you the user, e.g. your email address, name, company name, and telephone number, and therefore has been provided by you with your consent.   • To notify you about any changes to our website, such as improvements or service/product changes, that may affect our service.

Your personal information may be revealed to third parties under the following circumstances:
