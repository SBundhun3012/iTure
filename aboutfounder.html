Shailendrasingh Bundhun

The Founder

Mr. Shailendrasingh Bundhun, is a software engineer, with an engineering degree in Information systems obtained from the University of Mauritius. New technologies and gadgets have always been his passion, that led him paving his way to the IT sector, hence working for two major multinationals since the last five years.

Cumulating to five years of experience, Mr. Shailendrasingh Bundhun has been exposed to various enterprise-level products such as SAP products (HANA, Business Object XI3, Business Warehouse, and Analytical Cloud), to convey complex business solutions to influential clients in the sector of construction & energy in France.

He is distinctly experienced in the Business Intelligence field. He is also versatile in the Support and Maintenance areas for customized applications (LIMS – Laboratory Information Management System). Besides technical skills, he developed managerial and upstanding communication skills while having to deal with European and American clients on their daily business tasks. As he is born in Mauritius and is multilingual, he is apt to communicate in two major spoken languages; English and French.  This ability has been a plus factor in his professional environment.

After years of nurturing a vision to be a successful entrepreneur and as being passionate about technology, he founded his own company, iTure Ltd, located in the United Kingdom. iTure Ltd is deemed as being just a glint to his ultimate goal. His vision is to become an influential and contributing member to the IT sector with innovative ideas and new business approaches, blending into the upcoming panoply of technologies.
